According to the experts of IA "APK-Inform", the press service of the company "Protein Production", which is part of the Group Creative, this week the company has commissioned a powerful dryer capacity enables it to receive up to 2 thousand tons of soybeans per day, which gives to significantly minimize downtime of vehicles. This facility is part of the elevator complex with a capacity of simultaneous storage of 60 thousand. Tons of soybeans, launched "Protein Production" in Kirovograd region in August of this year

According to the press service, the introduction of a new dryer into operation is especially important at the final stage of harvesting soybean campaign in Ukraine, as market participants have reported a relatively high number of low-quality proposals oilseed. "However, the main complaints about the quality are high humidity beans and low in protein. In particular it should be noted that moisture content of soybeans, the proposed farmers, often varies within 14-18%. In some cases, market participants reported that humidity soybeans reaches 25% in addition, the company experts note a significant disadvantage storage space, capable of drying the beans and their proper storage, "-. According to the press-service.
